My Services

DevOps

"DevOps represents a change in IT culture, focusing on rapid IT service delivery through the adoption of agile, lean practices in the context of a system-oriented approach" - Gartner

DevOps solutions automate away inefficiencies and improve the quality and reliability of software. Kubernates, Terraform, Ansible, ArgoCD are just few devops tools that I use in this process.

Cloud

Start/migrate your project to the cloud and transfer the responsibility of running on-premises hardware and software to a specialist provider like Azure, GCP or AWS.

Even if on-premise solutions were less expensive, the steady and predictable cost of cloud solutions means no sudden, large, capital outlay that can strain the budget in an upgrade year.

Platform Engineer

In other words, we can say the platform engineering team serves the organization by planning, designing, and managing its cloud platforms.
